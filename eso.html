About ESO

The Elder Scrolls Online is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed by ZeniMax Online Studios. Set in the continent of Tamriel, it features both PvP and PvE content, as well as rich lore, dungeons, and a robust crafting system.
